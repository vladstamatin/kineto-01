Product Brief: Kinetotherapy & Medical Recovery Website

Role & Objective

    The website serves as the primary digital authority and conversion engine for "REBALANCE," a premium medical recovery clinic. Its dual objective is to establish immediate trust through demonstrated expertise and to streamline the user journey from "I have pain" to "Appointment Confirmed."

Design System & Aesthetic

    Theme: "Clinical Serenity." A fusion of high-end medical credibility with human warmth.
    Palette: Calming Sage Green (healing), Deep Slate Blue (trust), and Soft Cream (warmth) against plenty of whitespace.
    Typography: Clean, approachable sans-serifs (e.g., Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans) for maximum readability.
    Imagery: Authentic photography of therapy in progress (hands-on manual therapy, modern equipment usage) rather than generic stock medical photos.

Target Audience

    Post-Op Patients: Seeking specialized rehabilitation.
    Office Professionals: 25-45 y/o dealing with "tech neck" and lumbar issues.
    Active Seniors: Seeking mobility maintenance.

Content & Functionality

    Core Structure: Main Hero section (Value Prop), Services and prices (Kinetotherapy, Manual Therapy, Physiotherapy), Team (Credentials focus), and Footer wich includes Contact.
    Key Features: Sticky "Book Appointment" CTA on mobile, condition-specific landing pages (e.g., "Back Pain Solutions"), and embedded Google Maps/Reviews.

SEO & Localization

    Target Region: Moldova.
    SEO: Optimization for local intent keywords (e.g., "medical recovery near me," "herniated disc treatment"). Implementation of Schema.org markup for MedicalBusiness.
    Localization: Structure prepared for dual-language toggle (Local Language / English) to accommodate diverse local demographics.
    Languages: Optimize metadata and structure for search terms in both Romanian and Russian.
